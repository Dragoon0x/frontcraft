# Disclaimer

This project is experimental and provided as-is for educational and development purposes. The rules and thresholds are based on widely accepted standards including WCAG 2.1/2.2, Material Design 3, Apple Human Interface Guidelines, and Nielsen Norman Group research, but they represent general best practices, not absolute requirements.

Do Your Own Research (DYOR). Every project has unique constraints. Apply these rules with judgment, not dogmatically.

The npm utilities are validation helpers, not guarantees of compliance. Passing all checks does not constitute legal accessibility compliance. Consult accessibility professionals for formal WCAG audits.

This project is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with any of the organizations whose research is referenced (W3C, Google, Apple, Nielsen Norman Group, etc.).
